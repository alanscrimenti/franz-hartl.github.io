Levels of agreement were assessed on a scale from -2 (disagree) to 2 (agree) with the 0 point marked "in between."

On this scale, the mean level of agreement with the claim that chairman helped the environment unintentionally was 1.5.

This level of agreement is significantly higher than that obtained in the lottery condition, , t(53) = -5, p < .001, indicating that subjects truly regarded this behavior as unintentional.

The mean level of agreement with the claim that the chairman helped the environment intentionally was -1.3.
