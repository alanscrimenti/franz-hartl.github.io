Levels of agreement were assessed on a scale from -2 (disagree) to 2 (agree) with the 0 point marked "in between."

On this scale, the mean level of agreement with the claim that chairman harmed the environment intentionally was 1.0.

This level of agreement is significantly higher than that obtained in the lottery condition, , t(54) = -3.3, p < .001, indicating that subjects truly regarded this behavior as intentional.

The mean level of agreement with the claim that the chairman harmed the environment unintentionally was -1.5.
