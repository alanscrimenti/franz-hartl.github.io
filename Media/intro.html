Joshua Knobe

 
 

I’m a professor at Yale University, who works on questions at the intersection of philosophy and psychology. Most of my work is part of a new movement called ‘experimental philosophy’ that tries to go after the traditional questions of philosophy by conducting experimental studies to see how ordinary people actually think and feel.


If you are interested in experimental philosophy, you can try taking a look at the Experimental Philosophy Page or at the New York Times article describing the movement as a whole.


Or if you are interested in my more academic work, you can go to my official academic website.


Most importantly, if you are interested in the indie rock stylings of my beloved wife Alina Simone, you should definitely check out her new page.
