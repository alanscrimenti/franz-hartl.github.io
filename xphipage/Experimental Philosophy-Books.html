Books
 The Experimental Philosophy Site

Advances in Experimental Philosophy of Mind. Ed. Justin Sytsma. (Bloomsbury, 2014).

A collection of new papers in experimental philosophy of mind.

Advances in Experimental Epistemology. Ed. James Beebe. (Bloomsbury, 2014).

A collection of new papers in experimental epistemology.

Advances in Experimental Moral Psychology. Eds. Hagop Sarkissian & Jennifer Cole Wright. (Bloomsbury, 2014).

A collection of new papers in experimental moral psychology.

Philosophical Methodology: The Armchair or the Laboratory?. Ed. Matthew Haug. (Routledge, 2013).

A collection of papers on philosophical methodology across major areas of philosophy.

Current Controversies in Experimental Philosophy. Ed. Edouard Machery. (Routledge, 2014).

A volume of original essays on four central topics in experimental philosophy: language, consciousness, free will and responsibility, and epistemology and the reliability of intuitions.

Experimental Philosophy: Volume 2. Eds. Joshua Knobe and Shaun Nichols. (Oxford University Press, 2013).

A second collection of important papers in experimental philosophy.

Joshua Alexander. Experimental Philosophy: An Introduction. (Polity, 2012).

An introduction to experimental philosophy for a general reader.

Philosophy: Traditional and Experimental Readings. Eds. Fritz Allhoff, Ron Mallon and Shaun Nichols. (Oxford University Press, 2012).

The first introductory philosophy reader to integrate cutting-edge work in empirical and experimental philosophy with traditional philosophy.

Experimental Philosophy and its Critics. Eds. Joachim Horvath and Thomas Grundmann. (Routledge, 2012).

A collection of essays discussing experimental philosophy and its relevance to traditional philosophy. Originally published as a special issue of Philosophical Psychology.

Experimental Philosophy. Eds. Joshua Knobe and Shaun Nichols. (Oxford University Press, 2008).

A collection of important papers in experimental philosophy.



Foreign Language Books

Florian Cova. Qu'en pensez-vous? Introduction à la philosophie expérimentale. (Germina, 2011).

An introduction to experimental philosophy (in French).

Ruwen Ogien. L'influence de l'odeur des croissants chauds sur la bonté humaine. (Grasset, 2011).

A book addressing a variety of questions in the experimental philosophy of ethics (in French).

Related Books

Kwame Anthony Appiah. Experiments in Ethics. (Harvard University Press, 2008).

A series of lectures relating moral psychology to philosophical ethics.

Shaun Nichols. Sentimental Rules: On the Natural Foundations of Moral Judgment. (Oxford University Press, 2007).

An extended argument that morality is psychologically based on a combination of sentiments and rules.

Moral Psychology, Volume 1: The Evolution of Morality: Adaptations and Innateness, Moral Psychology, Volume 2: The Cognitive Science of Morality: Intuition and Diversity, and Moral Psychology, Volume 3: The Neuroscience of Morality: Emotion, Brain Disorders, and Development. Ed. Walter Sinnott-Armstrong. (Bradford Books, MIT Press, 2007).

A collection of recent research on three different facets of moral psychology.
