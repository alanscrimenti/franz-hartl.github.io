Folk Psychology and Folk Morality:
Response to Critics

Forthcoming in Journal of Theoretical and Philosophical Psychology

Read the full paper as a pdf file.

Responds to six critics of my argument that moral judgments sometimes serve as an input to folk psychology.

Before reading this paper, it might be helpful to take a look at the paper to which the critics are responding (Knobe 2003).

Note: In the course of this paper, I discuss a number of unsolved problems. If you have any ideas about how to solve these problems, please don't hesitate to contact me.
